Overview

Ora is a Catholic liturgical prayer app designed to help you pray the Divine Office, follow the liturgical calendar, and access daily Mass readings. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information. Ora is a completely offline-first app that works without an internet connection.

Data Storage

  • All liturgical data (saints, Mass readings, prayers) is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No user accounts required

  • You can disable this permission at any time in iOS Settings

Notifications (Optional)

  • If you enable prayer reminders, local notifications are scheduled on your device
  • No push notifications or external servers involved
  • You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings
