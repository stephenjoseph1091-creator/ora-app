# Privacy Policy for Ora

**Last Updated:** January 15, 2026

## Overview

Ora is a Catholic liturgical prayer app designed to help you pray the Divine Office, follow the liturgical calendar, and access daily Mass readings. Your privacy is important to us.

## Information We Collect

**We collect NO personal information.** Ora is a completely offline-first app that works without an internet connection.

### Data Storage
- All liturgical data (saints, Mass readings, prayers) is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have servers)
- No analytics or tracking
- No user accounts required

### Optional Permissions

If you choose to enable these features:

**Calendar Access (Optional)**
- If you enable "Sync to iOS Calendar," the app will create a local calendar on your device called "Catholic — Liturgical"
- This data stays on your device and syncs via your iCloud account (if you use iCloud)
- We never see or access this data
- You can disable this permission at any time in iOS Settings

**Notifications (Optional)**
- If you enable prayer reminders, local notifications are scheduled on your device
- No push notifications or external servers involved
- You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings

## Data We Don't Collect

- ❌ No email addresses
- ❌ No names or contact information
- ❌ No location data
- ❌ No usage analytics
- ❌ No advertising identifiers
- ❌ No third-party tracking
- ❌ No cookies or web tracking

## Third-Party Services

Ora uses NO third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

Ora is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Your iOS device encryption protects your data
- We have no servers to be hacked
- No data breaches are possible from our side

## Your Rights

You have complete control:
- All data is stored locally on your device
- Delete the app to remove all data
- No account to delete (we don't have accounts)
- No "right to be forgotten" needed (we never remembered you)

## Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy if we ever add features that collect data. Check this page periodically for updates.
